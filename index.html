How I Work

Design Process

A research-driven, accessibility-first process refined across consulting and agency environments.

🔍
01 · Discover

Discover

🎯
02 · Define

Define

Personas, problem framing and success metrics — including accessibility requirements — defined with cross-functional teams.

🎨

Design

Deliver

About Me

I'm a Senior Product & UX/UI Designer with over 7 years of experience driving user-centered, visually impactful digital products across diverse industries. My path has taken me from Design Systems at Jakala to leading UX and UI design across web and mobile platforms at PwC.
